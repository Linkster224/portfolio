The Blueberry Stand.

The Blueberry Stand is a Discord Bot Hosting platform,
connected with a dedicated/supporting community,

We're making it easy for users to host,
manage, and troubleshoot their bots.

Whether you're running a small personal bot,
or a larger community project, the platform is
designed to provide reliable hosting, while offering
the tools and guidance needed to keep your bot
online and performing well.

Beyond hosting, The BlueBerry stand is home
to an active support server, where users can
ask questions, report issues, share ideas, and
connect with other developers.

With a focused on friendly support and
dependable service, the community aims
to make Bot Hosting approachable for
beginners, while still providing the
resources experienced developers need.
